Homeschooling your child can seem daunting - but don't worry, you can do it! There are tons of programs and readable material available that will not only get you started, but carry you all of the way through this wonderful journey.

It's important to remember that this is a unique opportunity to connect with your child - most children do not get the constant one-on-one attention that homeschooling provides! But also remember that boundaries need to be set in place.

Don't forget to play & craft! Most kids love crafting - and what kid doesn't want to play? This is also another wonderful way to not only get some fun time in, but also use it in a constructive and educational way.

Don't be afraid to encourage alone play time for your child. Homeschooling has a lot of advantages, but it also has it's disadvantages - one of them being, when do you two get time away from each other? I mean, you can't exactly run away from your child can you?? You can use some of the time during the day, maybe even just 30 minutes to 1 hour, of "alone" play time for your child. You can help them understand that it's okay to play by themselves by engaging in something yourself as well, like reading a book.
